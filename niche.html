plant advice

the next time you go olallieberry-picking, make sure to follow these simple instructions. firstly, obviously don't pick the red ones. olallieberries are black. that also means that you have to pay close attention not to pick the dark purple ones. you have to look at the berries under the light and find the ones that are the darkest shade black. in terms of size, opt for berries that are not shriveled up or bursting from juice. the shriveled up ones will taste bitter. the overly juicy ones will not have prime olallieberry flavor. thank you for supporting your local farmers.

gardener tip: if you are on a walk around town and you really like one of your neighbor's plants, snap off a sprig. before planting it, snap off all of the leaves (or at least all of the big ones) to ensure better growth.

liam james does good stuff



have you heard of liam james? of course not, that's why i'm here. i am here to take you on a trip through the adorable and the awkward. if you have either heard of or watched psych, liam james is the sweet little kid that plays young shawn spencer. but the real reason i gathered you all here today is to talk about his best movie, the way way back. if you have ever felt lonely while being bullied by a popular blonde girl at your mother's boyfriend's beach house, you will like this movie. basically, there's a young teenager who is staying the summer and is constanly belittled by the mother's terrible boyfriend. he is sad and awkward and can't talk to girls, but one day he meets a young lifeguard who treats him right and invited him to come work at the water park. he sneaks off to the waterpark everyday, and soon enough, he is transformed into a new human being. wonderful. it has all the makings of a classic movie: good music, blue-ass water, and a nice sam rockwell with snazzy sunglasses. that's all i have to say. liam james is pretty cool. definitely watch the way way back if you get a chance.

some very distinct people


a few months ago, near school, i saw a very distinct guy. he reminded me of jason dean (jd) from heathers. he was walking around with his girlfriend (i assume), who had bleached hair and a punk outfit. however, he was the most distinct. it was summer weather outside, and he was wearing a black floor-length trenchcoat, black jeans, and black docs. his shirt was also black, but it also incorporated some color as it had WRATH printed in red capital letter. he was very distinct.

then, just this morning, i saw a very distinct guy on bart. he was dressed in business casual all black, with a single silver hoop earring and a silver hoop nose ring. he has short, almost military-style hair that he would not stop touching and running his fingers through. very strange. then, when we were leaving bart at embarcadero, he sprinted (yes, sprinted) up three flights of stairs. he was also very distinct.
